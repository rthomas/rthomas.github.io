About

Ryan has been writing code in a professional capacity for over 10 years, having worked in both start-ups and large enterprise he now loves working at Atlassian in Sydney, Australia.

Most of Ryans career has been focussed on developing web-applications in Java, but the last couple of years have been spent developing tools in a multitude of languages to deliver, scale and manage applications across large multi-datacentre deployments.

Ryan enjoys leading and motivating teams and delivering large, high-impact projects.

In his down-time, Ryan enjoys spending time with his family, learning new languages (Haskell is the current one), building RC aircraft and mountain biking when he can find the time.
